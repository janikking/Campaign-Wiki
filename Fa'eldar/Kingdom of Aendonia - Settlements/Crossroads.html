_A decently sized town located central in Aendonia, on the four way crossing to the central roads of the kingdom. It’s is a heavily agricultural-focused town, being the main producer of grains and wheats for the rest of the kingdom and then some. Those not into growing crops will likely find themselves in the business of brewing ales or distilling liquors. _

General

Third major city in the kiingdom of Aendonia, perfectly central to all the other settlements. Major farming town, providing grains for most of the kingdom as well as for export. A lot of travellers come through because of the location. A lot of farmers but also filled with traders, craftsmen, tavernkeeps etc as there is a lot of traffic coming through the city Political leader is a adjacent to the city guard captain, having ties to all the guild leaders in the city. Main sources of labour are farming the lands, providing places for dcrinks and shit, and crafts to sell to travellers and to keep the farming going. The main deitic worshop goes to Bahamut and Pelor The city guard has a large presence here although it has deteriorated in the last decade

An extra 30 gold per proof of slain gnoll, by providing sets of ears.
