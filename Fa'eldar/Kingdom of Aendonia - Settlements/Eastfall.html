One of the smallest, most remote villages of the Kingdom of Aendonia. Located between the Crying Cliffs and Aenden’s Domain, the weather is not the most favourable nor is the fertility of the soil. The reason people first started gathering around the area and why it is still a profitable location is due to the proximity to the bountiful gemstone veins under the mountain

Country: Aendonia Region: Western Cliffs at the Fool’s Ocean Population: ~1300, mostly dwarven Primary Deities: Moradin, the Allhammer, Erathis, the Lawbearer Goods produced: Gems (Emeralds, Opals, Rubies, Diamonds, Topaz), Ores, Coal, Stone

General { .hoofd}

  • City with a lot of dwarves, a lot of which are currently being forced to dig tunnels under Aendens Domain. Started as a mining town, which is why a lot of dwarves decided to inhabit eastfall.
  • Currently the one controlling eastfall is Soragil, an Erinye, currently holding the alias of Michelle Forestorm, the trusted and persuasive foreman of the mine. Has most of the population in the palm of ther hand.
  • Main trade is mining for gemstones and valuable stones/rocks, some of which get processed in the village, but also metals. Currently mainly diggin a deep mine
  • Moradin is the most worshipped deity in the village due to the dwarven population.
  • There’s few guards but a number of muscle under the command of Soragil.
